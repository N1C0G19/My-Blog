Movie Review

Pokemon the Movie 3: The Spell of the Unknown
Great movie for all pokemon fans

The movie is about Ash and his friends trying to stop Entei, a legendary pokemon turned evil, from destroying Proffesor Spencer Hale and his daughter, Molly Hale in the demension of a pokemon called Unknown. The proffesor got sucked into the dimension when he was exploring some ruins. The movie is full of suprises that make you want more and more of it.
